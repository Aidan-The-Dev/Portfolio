About Me

I'm Aidan White, an 18-year-old full-stack developer from the United States. I began coding at the age of 11, starting with JavaScript, and later diving into HTML and CSS. Today, I create websites and discord bots.

I believe in the power of web development to bring ideas to life. My goal is to make websites that not only look good but also serve a meaningful purpose. Well-designed websites can enhance how people interact with the digital world, and that's what drives my passion for web development.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy gaming and listening to music. These interests often inspire my creative solutions and ideas in coding projects.

I'm also a Discord server moderator, honing my interpersonal and problem-solving skills while fostering positive online communities.

Technology is ever-evolving, and I'm determined to contribute to its transformation. Whether it's a personal project or a client's, my work reflects my dedication, attention to detail, and a constant desire to improve.

If you're interested in discussing a website project, a custom Discord bot, or just want to connect, feel free to reach out. You can email me at aidanthedev@zohomail.com or use the Contact page in the navigation bar. I'm always eager to explore new opportunities and collaborate with those who share a passion for technology and innovation.
